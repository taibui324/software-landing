# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
SMB founders, operators, and team leads who know their current workflows are too manual and need a practical software partner. They are evaluating whether Orca can understand messy operations, design the right system, and ship useful software without turning the project into a long consulting maze.

## Product Purpose
Orca is a software consulting company that builds custom software, AI automations, workflow integrations, and internal tools for growing teams. The landing page should make Orca feel credible, fast, and operationally grounded, then move qualified visitors toward booking a call.

## Brand Personality
Sharp, practical, fast. Orca should feel like a technical operator with taste: direct, confident, specific, and calm under pressure.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic SaaS templates, over-decorated AI agency pages, fake futuristic dashboards, vague transformation copy, endless identical card grids, and black-and-white minimalism that feels unfinished rather than intentional.

## Design Principles
- Show working systems, not abstract promises.
- Make the offer legible in one pass: software, AI automation, internal tools.
- Use contrast, motion, and product-like artifacts to create proof without clutter.
- Keep copy specific and operational, with every sentence tied to a team problem or outcome.
- Preserve a crisp monochrome identity, but use amber signal accents for momentum, status, and attention.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences for marquee, reveal, typing, carousel, and decorative animation. Keep headings readable on mobile, avoid text overflow, provide semantic sections and labels, and ensure controls have clear accessible names.
